Cycle-level lesson sharpened: wrong/right contrasts work when the
model's prior *disagrees* with the wrong form (it never wanted to flip
arguments once told), and backfire when the wrong form matches a
behavior the model already prefers (defensive guards) — in that case
the concrete string is a template, not a warning.
